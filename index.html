BS INFORMATION TECHNOLOGY

Designed to provide a broad understanding of information and communication technology, our BSIT program delivers an innovative education that equips students with practical knowledge and real-life skills to adapt in a rapidly evolving ICT-driven world. Grasp various technical expertise from cloud computing, mobile & web development, cybersecurity, user experience design to information management, and get industry-standard certifications from our global partners including Huawei, Microsoft, and Oracle to name a few.

Who We Are

Shaping Leaders of Tomorrow

Founded in 1983, STI has grown into one of the Philippines' largest educational institutions with over 120 campuses and 40,000+ students nationwide.

Our programs bridge academic learning with industry requirements — equipping graduates with the skills and confidence to compete globally.

STI's E2E (Enrollment to Employment) system supports every student from admission through career placement.
